How to Play

Engineered for litter environments
  • Move the OdorSol bottle left and right along the floor.
  • Spray upward to zap odor clouds rising from the litter boxes before they escape into the house.
  • Know your odors: puffy green = normal stink, spiky yellow = fast ammonia, swirly purple = zigzagging mystery stink, big red = mega multi-cat odor (needs two hits!).
  • Don't spray the couch, carpet roll, cat toy, or food bowl on the sides — OdorSol is for cat litter only, and wrong targets cost points.
  • Your cat is a friend, not a target. Watch it cheer when you clear the air nearby!
  • Keep Freshness above zero. Escaped clouds drain the meter — at 0% it's game over.
  • Catch falling power-ups: OdorSol Boost, Fresh Home Shield, Scoop Bonus, and the chain-reacting Oxidation Wave.
